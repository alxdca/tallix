# Archive and Past Years

Tallix keeps historical budgets and transactions by year. The sidebar includes an Archive section with past years.

## Viewing past years

- Click a year in the Archive section to open that year in the selected view.
- Opening an archived year does not change the current year at the top of the menu.

## What is archived

- Transactions for that year.
- Budget categories and planned values.
- Asset and debt values per year.

## Tips

- Use archive views to review spending patterns year over year.
- Keep the current year focused on ongoing tracking.
